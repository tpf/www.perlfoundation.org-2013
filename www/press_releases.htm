The following Press releases may be used to discuss the events of The Perl Foundation. If you need to discuss usage of the information, copyright notice, language use and quotation then please contact pr(at)perlfoundation.org.

If you are writing a story about Perl, scripting languages, virtual machines, open source software, etc? We're happy to assist. Contact us at press(at)perl.org

If you are wishing to convert these files to another language, further add to the list or help in the creation of press material please contact Mark Keating (mdk on irc, mdk(at)perlfoundation.org). Materials are also duplicated on the tpf git repo: https://github.com/tpf/marketing-materials.

Flyers for Distribution

These flyers are all A5 and can be printed in colour or black and white or mailed as pdf files/converted to graphics and included in websites. If you wish to discuss usage of the flyers contact pr(at)perlfoundation.org. If you would like to print these out on a home, office, or home-office printer (non-commercial printer) then you can use the variant files labeled as "home-office". Any variant named with the word "bleed" has a 3mm bleed area so can be printed either digitally or lithographically.
